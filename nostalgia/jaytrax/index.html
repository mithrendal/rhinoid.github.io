2000 - Jaytrax (PC)

In my own time I ’re-imagined’ my Amiga music program Mugician, for the PC. The PC had much more processor power so in theory a lot more could be possible. Jaytrax had 16 channels of audio, a new synth engine (loosely based on Mugician), and new demo songs to show off the possibilities. The app never really went anywhere until I converted it to the PocketPC and Mobile phones. (See the section about ‘Syntrax’).

Big ideas for small devices.
