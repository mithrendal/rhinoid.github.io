1987 - Bouncer (Amiga)

The first game I’ve ever made was called Bouncer, and it was made for the Amiga. I made it together with a friend called Thijs van Rijswijk and another friend of mine Pieter Opdam made the graphics. Let’s be hounest, it was a pretty poor game, but at the time we were quite happy with it. As with everything back in those days it was all done in 68000 assembly.

The game was published by Turtle Byte in 1987, a publisher with bad (to no) ethics.

Big ideas for small devices.
