2003 - 180 darts (Symbian/Mobile)

While working at Overloaded (a mobile games company) I created 180 darts for Symbian devices (Nokia, SonyEricsson, etc). The game featured 2 player mode via bluetooth. It also used an oldskool pixelzoomer effect to follow the dart while it went into the board. (Phones didn’t have OpenGL or hardware graphis support yet)

Big ideas for small devices.
