Reinier van Vliet - portfolio

2005 - Adidas (J2me/Mobile/Ad)

While working at Overloaded (a mobile games company) together with Ferry Spiering I programmed the Adidas Advergame for j2me devices (Nokia, SonyEricsson, Samsung, Siemens, etc). The gameplay was based on an advertisement which was airing around that time of a guy running through the night.

Graphics were made by Edouard Albert, Dan Parks & Matthew Groen

Big ideas for small devices.
