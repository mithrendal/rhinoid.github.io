1988 - Iceball (Amiga)

The second game I’ve ever made was called Iceball, and it was made for the Amiga by the people who made the previous game ‘Bouncer’: Thijs van Rijswijk helped programming and another friend of mine Pieter Opdam made the graphics. Although the game was not as bad as Bouncer, it was also not very good. Still we learned a lot and it was the first of the many platform games I would work on.

The game was published by Turtle Byte in 1988, a pretty bad publisher. But that was the norm back then.
