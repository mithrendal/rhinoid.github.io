1993 - Cognition (Amiga)

In 1993 we (Metin Seven 🎨, Ramon Braumuller 🎵 and me 👨‍💻) were asked by Divo publishing (The publisher of ‘Amiga magazine’) to create a game for them. We came up with a game where you need to turn some cogs in such a way that the colors on them line up. Unfortunately some of the wheels are missing a few of their teeth. Confused?
