2016 - 2016 BLE secure beacon library - Clippon (iOS)

Clippon was a start-up focusing on secure beacons for various applications like vending machines, fleet control, etc.

Created a Bluetooth Low Energy library for them to aid app developers to connect to Clippon beacons in a secure way and optionally perform a transaction.

Big ideas for small devices.
