2018 - 2018 Volksbank (iOS)

I worked a few months on the birth of the ‘sl!m app’ from the Volksbank in a small dedicated team. Sl!m is an app where they want to experiment with new innovative features for their banking product, without immediatly giving it to their entire user base. It’s like a playground for new ideas which everyone can already try.

In the short time I was there I worked with the team on giving insight in to the payments of users and building a conversational interface (So like whatsapp, but then scripted(where the scripts where frontend hosted))
